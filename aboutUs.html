About Us





Metea Valley Robotics is split into 7 different teams. Each team, made up of 5-6 members, work together throughout each year to build, code, test, and compete with a robot.

Our Coaches

Mr. Schey, with us since 2014, built the groundwork for Metea Valley's Robotics program and led our teams to success.
Having a variety of technical skills, he helps students design, program, and fix any problems that arise along the way.

















Mr. Romeo, with us since 2016, finds his enjoyment in the world of computers. Although not knowing too much
on how to code a robot, he has the passion and dedication to learn and help our students! Nonetheless, he has led
our teams to multiple achievements, starting his first year leading one of our teams to the Create U.S. Open event.

This year, we have introduced mentors to the Metea Valley Robotics team. Our mentors are experienced veterans of the team, and they are permitted to join the meetings and see what each team's plan is for the season. Students are also able to ask them for guidance with certain things such as build designs and programming.
