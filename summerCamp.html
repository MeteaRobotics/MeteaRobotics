Summer Camp





What do We do?

Here at Metea Valley Robotics' Summer Camp, middle-schoolers in District 204 interested in the world of robotics are given the remarkable opportunity to learn and experience their passion for robotics up close with our high-schoolers. This two week camp provides middle school students with a chance to play the brand new game for the 2020-2021 Vex Robotics season. The students, split into teams of 5-6, are given a two-week period to build a robot with the guidance of 2-3 high-school student coaches working with their team. The students are given 5 days to build, 2 days to program, 1 day to test, and 2 days to compete with the other teams at the camp. The winning team can expect to win prizes, such as a Raspberry Pi. The Summer Camp runs from Monday, June 15th, to Friday, June 26th, from 1:30 P.M. to 5:30 P.M. at Metea Valley High School in Room C-124.

How can I join?

Students are able to join the Summer Camp by signing-up on the website. More information, as well as sign-up instructions, is given here: Metea Valley Robotics' Summer Camp for Middle and High-Schoolers
