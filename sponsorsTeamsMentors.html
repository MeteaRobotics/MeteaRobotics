Sponsors, Teams, and Mentors

Sponsors

Mr. Romeo and Mr. Schey are the sponsors of the Metea Valley Robotics Team. Mr. Romeo is a physics teacher and Mr. Schey is an engineering teacher and football coach. These two teachers help the students manage their time wiseley and offer advice when students ask. They schedule and enter our teams into competitions.

Teams

7 teams bear the Metea Valley 8995 Numbers: T, E, V, J, R, Z, and M. We involve both boys and girls in robotics. We have a freshman team, sophpomore team, junior team, senior team, girls team, and a mixed team.

Mentors

This year, we have introduced mentors to the Metea Valley Robotics team. Experienced robotics veterans that have been in our program can join as mentors and sit in on meetings. The students can ask for help with robot designs and programming.
