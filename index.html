Metea Valley
Robotics

Metea Valley High School, Aurora, Illinois, USA


Metea Valley's Robotics Program is an opportunity for all high-school students at Metea interested in the world of automation and robotics to pursue their passion. Our program requires students to exercise a variety of skills which includes building, programming, teamwork, positivity, and more. Every year, our students are challenged with a new competition, bringing out different and unique skills each year. Want to join? Conatct our coaches for more information!
