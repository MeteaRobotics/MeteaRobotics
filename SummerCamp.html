Summer Camp

What Do We Do?

Highschool students from district 204 coach middle schoolers who want to see what robotics is like. This two week camp will provide middle school students with a chance to play the brand new game for the 2020-2021 Vex Robotics season. We split the kids in the camp into teams of 5-6 each. They will have two weeks to build a robot with the guidance of 2-3 student coaches working with their team. The kids get 5 days to build, 2 days to program, 1 day to test, and 2 days to compete with the other teams at the camp. The winning team all got a free Raspberry Pi. The camp runs from Monday June 15th to Friday June 26th from 1:30 P.M. to 5:30 P.M. at Metea Valley High School in Room C-124.
